# BuildFlow AI - Responsible Disclosure Policy
#
# We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability:
# 1. Email security@buildflow-ai.app with details
# 2. Do NOT publicly disclose until we have had 90 days to remediate
# 3. Do NOT access, modify, or delete user data
# 4. Do NOT perform DoS attacks or disrupt service
#
# We commit to:
# - Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
# - Provide a resolution timeline within 7 days
# - Credit researchers in our security acknowledgements (if desired)
# - Not pursue legal action against good-faith researchers
#
